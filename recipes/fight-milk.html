The Gang's All Mighty Fight Milk

Description

This legendary concoction, rightly considered the ultimate fuel for champions, is the right choice for packing mass and bulking hard.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of vodka.
  • 2 quail eggs.
  • 1 cup of milk.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon.

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients into a vessel and shake as hard as possible.
  2. P.S: Using a blender is frowned upon by the fight gods and will result in a weaker milk.
  3. Pour and enjoy.
