Frank's Famous Rum Ham

Description

The holy rum ham, composed of the magical combination of rum and ham. A must-have for sailors, pirates, and anyone who just wants to be around lots of seamen.

Ingredients

  • 1 fully cooked ham, bone-in or boneless
  • 1 bottle of rum (dark or spiced)
  • Pineapple rings (optional, for garnish)
  • Maraschino cherries (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Remove any packaging or netting from the ham. If it has a layer of skin, leave it, it's extra protein.
  3. Place the ham in a roasting pan, fat side up.
  4. Pour rum over the ham.
  5. Cover the roasting pan tightly with aluminum foil or a lid to trap the moisture.
  6. Every 30 minutes, carefully remove the pan from the oven and baste the ham with the accumulated juices and rum.
  7. During the last 15 minutes of baking, you can add pineapple rings and maraschino cherries to the top of the ham for a classic touch.
  8. Let it rest and serve.
