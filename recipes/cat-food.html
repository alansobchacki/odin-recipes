Frank and Charlie's Beer-Scented Cat Food

Description

Brace yourself for a culinary experience that defies all expectations. This masterpiece manages to combine the most mundane ingredients in such a way that it induces a blissful state of utter satisfaciton and lethargy. With each bite, a wave of uneventfulness washes over you, making you feel as stuffed as a teddy bear after Thanksgiving dinner. It's a recipe that offers no excitement, no flavor, and no redeeming qualities — only the unparalleled power to ensure that you fall into a deep slumber faster than counting sheep. Bon appétit!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of cat food.
  • 1 can of beer.
  • 1 paper bag containing huffing glue.

Steps

  1. Open a can of beer and chug it as fast as you can.
  2. Open a can of cat food and again, swallow it as fast as possible. Using silverware is optional.
  3. Grab your paper bag filled with huffing glue and take a deep huff.
